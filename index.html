Why Auto Wrapz Inc?

Auto Wrapz Inc. is a certified installer if the Paint Protection Film (PPF or Clear Bra). Serving dealerships of the luxury, ultra luxury and performance segment of the automotive industry with such brands as Porshe, Bentley, Lamborghini, Rolls Royce, Ferrari and many more.

We provide the highest quality of PPF installation while utilizing specifically designed tools and advanced placement techniques to provide exceptional service to our customers.
